Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

Overview

MyIPTV Stream Player ("the App") is developed by Saed Zakari. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data stays on your device.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Playlist URLs that you add to the App
  • App preferences and settings

This data is not shared with anyone and is not accessible to the developer.

Network Usage

The App connects to the internet solely to fetch playlist data and stream video content from URLs that you provide. The App does not connect to any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at github.com/forever54321/iptv_app_ios.
